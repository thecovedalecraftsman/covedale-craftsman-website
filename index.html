About Christopher

A calm, detail-oriented craftsman with a background in aviation maintenance and a commitment to serving families in Greater Cincinnati.

I’m Christopher, the owner of The Covedale Craftsman. For 13 years I served in the Ohio Army National Guard as both an aircraft electrician and avionics technician working on Black Hawk helicopters — including a deployment to Syria.

That experience taught me the value of discipline, clear communication, and doing the job right the first time. Today, I bring those same standards into your home: showing up when I say I will, respecting your space, and focusing on clean, precise work.

When I’m not helping homeowners with projects, I’m with my family and building a business I’m proud of — one that treats people fairly and leaves every home better than I found it.
